<paragraph>
ToyLatex is minimalistic version of LaTeX, which is a document preparation system 
and document markup language. Latex is widely used for the communication and publication 
of scientific documents in many fields, including mathematics, physics, computer science, 
statistics, economics, and political science. Latex also has a prominent role in the preparation 
and publication of books and articles that contain complex multilingual materials, 
such as Sanskrit and Arabic, including critical editions. This text has been taken from wikipedia.
